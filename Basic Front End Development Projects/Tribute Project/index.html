Ronnie O'Sullivan

"The Rocket"


Brief summary about Ronnie's snooker career:

  • Born on 5 December 1975
  • Professional snooker player, turned pro in 1992
  • Nickname: The Rocket
  • Ronnie is a five times World Champion. He has won 27 ranking titles, including 5 UK Championships, 3 minor ranking titles and 27 invitational professional tournaments, among them 5 Masters titles.
  • His entertaining, fast and attacking style of play earned him his nickname, The Rocket.
  • Ronnie is the author of two books, published by Orion: "Ronnie, the Autobiography of Ronnie O'Sullivan" (2003) and "Running: The Autobiography" (2013).
  • Ronnie is also an avid runner and takes part in marathons regularly.
  • He recently also made a show where he tried his luck to hustle best pool players across America.
